Why is Gardening good for you?

 

Gardening can be very good for you

You can burn up to 300 calories during just one hour of gardening and yard work

 

Gardening can also be good for your health

Just 30 minutes of gardening a few times a week can prevent and control high blood pressure. It scored very high on the Natural Heart, Lung and Blood Institutes recommendation list for battling high blood pressure.

 

While your out gardening in the sun, you also soak up plenty of vitamin D. Vitamin D helps the body absorb calcium, In turn calcium helps keep the your bones strong and healthy.

Source: Better Health Victoria
